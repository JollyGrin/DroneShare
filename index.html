What's going on here?

NestEgg is exploring how to collectivize ownership in value producing infrastructure, to enable anyone to secure their future with the basic services they need. Achieving this end-vision requires three components:

Crowdfunding an asset into existence
Collective Ownership
Distributing the utility created by the asset

NestEgg is building small experiments to figure out the road (and improve along it) to our future product/proposition. Therefore, we've designed the DroneShare to test out the logistics for managing collective ownership. Here's what you can expect from participating in this experiment.


Phase 1: Signup and Crowdfund

Participants start the experiment by purchasing ownership in the asset. This is accomplished by users signing up for the experiment and depositing ETH into the crowdfund address, which will trigger NestEgg to send tokens to their wallet.


Phase 2: Shared Ownership

During the experiment, both shareholders and non-shareholders will be able to rent drones. Shareholders get 1st priority for reservations and receive free rental days in proportion to their ownership (for example: if you hold 10% of ownership, you’ll also receive 10% of the total days) Non-shareholders will pay a standard rental fee. NestEgg collects this fee, saves it for possible repairs/replacements and if not used, sends the profit at the end of the experiment to shareholders in proportion to their ownership (for example: if a non-shareholder pays €10 to rent a drone for 2 days, a shareholder with 10% of the fleet would receive €1).

Shareholders will receive DAY tokens in proportion to their ownership, delivered to the same address where they hold ownership, which they can use to rent a drone for a day. This makes the shareholder's free rental transferrable and trackable.

Phase 3: Closing Auction

This DroneShare is an experiment, and as such requires an end (as NestEgg cannot indefinitely service the experiment). Thus, at the end NestEgg will host an auction for anyone to purchase the drones. The money raised during these auctions will be distributed to shareholders in proportion to their ownership. Assuming a successful auction, shareholders will receive (a part of) their initial investment back. Read more about this on the tab 'rules'.

Rules of Game

NestEgg designed this DroneShare experiment to figure out pieces of the puzzle of the future product. This specific experiment is to test out the logistics for managing collective ownership. Here’s the ‘rules of the game’.

Sharing ownership = Shared responsibility

You’ll share the drone with other owners. Ownership grants you the right to use the drone for a specific amount of days in proportion to the ownership you’ve bought. Shared ownership also means shared responsibility. Please therefore treat the asset you take home as you want others to treat it. As an owner you are responsible for bringing the drone back in time, in order to make the drone available to your shared owners for other days. Therefore, we charge 10 euros for each day the drone is not returned in time.

Risk Insurance

Sharing ownership also means making appointments on who accounts for the risk of damage. After receiving customer feedback, NestEgg decided to offer an insurance on the drone share. Note that NestEgg offers this insurance informally. The token price therefore includes a share with insurance costs. When the drone gets damaged, the costs are first distracted from the insurance and if not sufficient, distracted from the money generated by single rents. If at the end of the experiment there is insurance money left, it is redistributed to the shareholders.

Revenue Distribution

Non-shareholders will pay a standard rental fee for the drone. NestEgg collects this fee and saves it for possible repairs/replacements and if not used, sends the profit to the shareholders.

Using your DAY Token

When you purchase a NestEgg Drone Ownership Token (OWN), that entitles you to 1 free rental day a month. After you purchase your OWN token, DAY tokens will be sent to the same wallet. You will receive 1 DAY token a month, for every OWN token you own.

Don't see DAY tokens in your wallet? Press "Load Token Balances" manually to refresh. Or simply view your address in an ethereum block explorer.



Reserving with your DAY token.

When you make a reservation, instead of paying €5 when picking up your drone, you can use your DAY token. After making your reservation, open your wallet and select "Send Ether & Tokens".

Send to the address 0x70E9c9F94850a875C82c2B75b9EE01fC994f5cc6. Send 1 DAY token per reservation day you'd like to rent.

Upon picking up your rental, we'll check your ether address to see if you're paid up.
